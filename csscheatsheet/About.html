Harle Brako History

Harle Brako Limited is a Ghanaian company established by Chales Brako in the year of 1964 as an expert in the field of industrial and enviromental cleaning.Harle Brako Company Limited has gain alot contract in several company within the country due to the best of services offered to their clients.His dedication to his work made him win the best cleaning contractor in kumasi in the late 60's.Mobil Oil Company offered him a contract where he also made the company win the most neatiest company in that year.He became a cement distributor at Ghacem Takoradi Branch and later won a cleaning bid at Ghacem to intensify their cleaning in the plant. He aggrandized this service and now is the best and well known cleaner at Ghacem Takoradi Branch and other company.
